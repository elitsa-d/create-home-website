Meet our key people

kerry
southern CEO & Creative Director

Kerry is an acclaimed Interior Designer, with a proud 100% record of on-time and on-budget project completion. She has an exceptional reputation with clients and suppliers alike. Personal experience of her Grandmother’s time in a dementia care home and her mother dying in a hospice have left her passionate about end-of-life interiors.

william
reason Head of Installations

William has overseen bulk installations for over fifteen years and his ‘Serve to Lead’ mentality is popular with clients and staff alike. His longstanding relationships with stakeholders in our industry are a testament to his ability to be the on-site conduit for the brand values of both The Care Home Interiors Company and any of our respective clients and/or partners.

chris
southern Technical Advisor

Chris has spent his entire working life in the construction industry, gaining a vast knowledge of different trades. He prides himself on his high standard of workmanship and has enjoyed learning how to overcome different and challenging fit outs. Friendly and easy-going, he enjoys working as part of the team and his experience is highly valued by all at The Care Home Interiors Company.

simon
martin Head of Production

Simon has a wealth of Financial and Business experience. He is a Chartered Accountant with the skill set to tackle new challenges head on and turn his hand to any aspect of the business. He works in close liaison with suppliers, Procurement and Installations to ensure the production line is well-stocked with raw materials as well as on track to meet its obligations to the Installations team.

jess
waters creative

Jess first came to work in what are now The Care Home Interior Company’s work rooms on her work experience placement from school. Acting on a natural talent for art and design, Jess discovered a prodigious ability to produce beautiful hand-made curtains. She also curates the production of lamp shades and bespoke multi fabric designed cushions, and works closely with the Creative Director and Head of Production.

mel
wood Head of Procurement

Mel oversees all The Care Home Interiors Company’s procurement. The component parts that make up each home can run into thousands. Mel has close relationships with all stakeholders ensuring all deadlines are met, stock counts are accurate and dispenses the careful administration of last minute tweaks to design.
