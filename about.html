About us

The Care Home Interiors company is the UK’s leading designer, manufacturer, wholesaler and installer of furnishings for the social care sector. World renowned for multi award winning turn key packages, it has a reputation for the longevity of their relationships with clients and suppliers alike. Proudly dedicated to UK manufacturing, the company creates most of its products on site and sources materials individually from UK artisans. Custodians of crafts deep in our heritage, the Care Home Interiors Company is proud of its approach.

Form and function

Our installations team has over 10 years’ worth of experience of working together. Our efficient, courteous and uniformed team of staff can install any Care Home project of any size. A typical project see us deliver an entire 70 bed turnkey package to a Care Home in two weeks – our team are unrivalled in their speed, reliability and professional installation of an entire fitout. Whether we are installing a single chair or entire home our staff have a great sense of pride in all we do.

Our ethos

The Care Home Interiors company is acutely aware that there is rarely a one size fits all solution when combining budget, durability and end design. As such we embrace the individual needs of each client and develop bespoke relationships accordingly. As primarily turnkey specialists, we expertly transform empty buildings into award winning designs as acclaimed at the Pinders Healthcare Design Awards and in the International Property Awards.

We love design

Through our partnerships with leading Interior Designers, we can manufacture bespoke item designs as well as source bespoke project Interior Design services. The experience shared between ourselves and our partners in every step of design, manufacturing, installation and support empowers you to develop your own bespoke relationship with the Care Home Interiors company. Our flexible business structure ensures we are always open to new forms of collaboration from within your own organisation.

Charity

Want to know more about our charitable donations? Our founder, Kerry Southern, explains why we support one special charity close to all our hearts here at the Care Home Interiors Company… St Michael’s Hospice.

During 2016, my mum Penny Southern was diagnosed with terminal cancer. She was a huge inﬂuence in my life and the company today. She trained many of our staff, had fabulous, long-lasting relationships with our suppliers and customers alike. Mum was lucky that in her time of need, St Michael’s Hospice were able to take her. Mum’s stay at St Michael’s was one of their longest until she passed away in March 2017. During that time, I realised that people were losing loved ones in places like this up and down the country.

I was shocked to learn that St Michael’s Hospice receives less than 20% of its funding from the North Hampshire Clinical Commissioning Group. The remaining £3.1m comes from donations.

Each month, we send an entire van of furniture and furnishings to its Hospice Shop to ensure it is fully stocked.

We are also helping to refurbish the Hospice, offering our services at no cost to help release their budget for the fantastic palliative care they offer their residents. At a most tragic, distressing period in life, St Michael’s Hospice provides great solace, warmth and a loving, homeliness to people who need it most.

Kerry Southern
